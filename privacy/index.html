Privacy Policy

Calculator inputs

Income, custody, and expense figures you enter into any calculator on this site are processed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. They are never sent to our servers, logged, or stored anywhere by us. Closing or reloading the page clears them.

Analytics and advertising

This site does not currently run advertising or analytics scripts. If that changes in the future, this policy will be updated to disclose exactly what's added and what data (if any) it collects — it will not retroactively apply to calculator inputs, which remain client-side by design regardless of what other scripts run on the page.
